a composer, sound artist, and audio designer, specialising in sounds for animated, interactive, and interdisciplinary works. I tend to focus on independent video games and animations, but I also enjoy working on installations and sound art, as well as collaborating with other artists (choreographers, dramaturgy, etc).

My works for games have been showcased at events such as PAX Prime, EGX Rezzed, and A MAZE Festival. I have also collaborated with a variety of creatives who have studied at reputable arts schools including but not limited to, Trinity Laban School of Dance, RMIT University, SVA, Animation Vieux Montreal, and CalArts.
